Do you wonder if Web Development is for you?

  • Passion for Technology: Are you genuinely interested in technology, including programming languages, frameworks, and emerging trends in web development?
  • Love for Design: Do you appreciate good design principles, including layout, typography, color theory, and user experience (UX) design?
  • Problem-Solving Skills: Are you adept at analyzing problems and finding efficient solutions, a critical skill in web development?
  • Attention to Detail: Do you pay attention to the finer details of code structure, user interface (UI) elements, and browser compatibility to ensure a polished final product?
  • Continuous Learning: Are you committed to ongoing learning and staying updated with new technologies, tools, and best practices in web development?
  • Communication Skills: Can you effectively communicate complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders, collaborate with team members, and provide clear documentation?
  • Adaptability: Are you flexible and open to learning new programming languages, frameworks, or methodologies as needed for different projects?
  • Creativity: Can you think creatively to find innovative solutions and bring unique perspectives to web development projects?

About Me

The Front End Web Developer

My journey into the world of software engineering began with a simple code challenge, igniting a passion that would reshape my career trajectory. In 2020, I took a leap of faith and enrolled in the UT Coding Bootcamp, drawn by its stellar reputation and promise of transformation. Over the course of six intense months, I immersed myself in a whirlwind of learning, both personal and academic, that left an indelible mark on my journey.

Balancing full-time work with bootcamp demands was no small feat, and while I missed out on intern opportunities, I emerged from the program with a newfound sense of self-discipline and a portfolio of projects showcasing my burgeoning coding skills. Yet, as I eagerly stepped into the job market in January 2021, I encountered the harsh reality of a competitive landscape. Undeterred, I persisted.

Life had other plans in store when a company merger altered my anticipated career trajectory. Rather than falter, I embraced the opportunity for growth and independence. Venturing into freelancing, I honed my craft while continuing to learn and evolve as a software developer.

Through every twist and turn, one thing remains unwavering: my unwavering determination to pursue my dream job as a software engineer. With each challenge, I emerge stronger, more resilient, and more committed than ever to realizing my aspirations in this dynamic field.
